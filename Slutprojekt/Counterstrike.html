Counter Strike

Counter:Strike is a computer game with five cops against five terrorists. A game is up to 30 rounds or the first team that reaches 16 rounds. The mission for the terrorists are to eliminate the cops or plant the bomb on one of the two bombsites. The mission for the cops are to either defuse the planted bomb or to eliminate the other team before the bomb goes off. Counter:Strike did start off with "Counter:Strike 1.5" and it was a game against bots. It was released on the 18th of June 1999.Further on it became "Counter:Strke 1.6" and you could challenge other players and tournaments was a thing. Counter:Strike did get famous at this time. "Counter:Strike Source" was released on the 16th of November 2004 and was a less success than Counter:Strike 1.6. Pros didn't like the game.

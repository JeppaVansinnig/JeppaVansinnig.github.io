In 2013 Virtus.Pro won the SLTV Starseries V. SLTV Starseries is a tournament arranged in Russia. The prize that was given out for Virtus.Pro was 3500 USD.
The five top teams was sharing an amout of 10000 USD.
Later that year they ended up in third spot in the Copenhagen Games. Copenhagen games is arranged in Denmark and is a smaller tournament than SLTV Starseries.
There they was given 1000USD and the top five teams shared an amount of 8500 USD.

On January 25, Virtus.pro signed the five member roster of AGAiN, Jarosław "pashaBiceps" Jarząbkowski, Janusz "Snax" Pogorzelski, Paweł "byali" Bieliński, and Golden Five players Wiktor "TaZ" Wojtas, Filip "Neo" Kubski. Virtus.pro won EMS One Katowice 2014 by beating Ninjas in Pyjamas in the finals. The team then got 5-8 at ESL One Cologne 2014.
